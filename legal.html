Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 21, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit Helium-3 Labs (the “Site”). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.


PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and other details you provide when contacting us or signing up for our services.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent on site, and browser type.
  • Device Information: IP address, operating system, and other technical details about the device you use to access our site.


HOW WE COLLECT INFORMATION

  • Direct Collection: Information you provide to us through forms or communications.
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device to remember your preferences and track your website usage
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and improve our service
  • Other Tracking Technologies: Web beacons, pixels, and similar technologies may be used to collect information about your interactions with our site


USE OF YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyze user behavior and preferences
  • Personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about our products and services
  • Comply with legal obligations


DATA SHARING AND THIRD PARTIES

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Analytics partners, including Google, to help us understand website usage
  • Legal authorities when required by law


YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing activities
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

OPT OUT OF TRACKING

  • You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • You may also adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, although this may impact your ability to use certain features of our website.


DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


CHANGES

We may update this privacy policy from time to time for personal, operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The latest version will always be available on our website.


CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
